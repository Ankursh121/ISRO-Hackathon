# Problem Statement 1 Analysis

## Title

Optimizing Urban Heat Mitigation and Cooling Strategies using Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AI/ML)

## Problem Statement

Urban areas often experience higher temperatures than nearby rural regions. This phenomenon, known as the Urban Heat Island (UHI) effect, occurs due to rapid urbanization, dense infrastructure, reduced vegetation cover, and heat-retaining surfaces such as concrete and asphalt.

The increasing intensity of urban heat negatively impacts public health, energy consumption, environmental quality, and overall urban sustainability.

## Objective

Develop an AI/ML-based geospatial system capable of:

* Identifying urban heat hotspots
* Analyzing factors contributing to urban heating
* Modeling urban heat dynamics
* Simulating cooling interventions
* Recommending optimal mitigation strategies

## Input Datasets

### Remote Sensing Data

* Landsat 8 Land Surface Temperature (LST)
* ECOSTRESS Land Surface Temperature
* Sentinel-2 Land Use / Land Cover Data

### Meteorological Data

* ERA5 Atmospheric Variables
* Air Temperature
* Humidity
* Wind Speed

### Urban Infrastructure Data

* OpenStreetMap
* Global Human Settlement Layer

## Expected Outputs

* Heat Stress Maps
* Urban Heat Hotspot Detection
* Urban Heat Driver Analysis
* Cooling Intervention Simulations
* Mitigation Recommendations
